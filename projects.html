What makes a project card strong

Keep the signal high and the wording precise.

Start with the problem

The reader should understand why the work mattered before they see the tools.

Name your contribution

Be explicit about your role, especially in team work or class projects.

Link to proof

Attach a repo, report, dashboard, or write-up whenever you can.

Portfolio Goal

Three strong entries are enough.

You do not need many projects. You need a few that communicate taste, judgment, and follow-through.
