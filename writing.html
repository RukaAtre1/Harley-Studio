Publishing Pattern

A repeatable structure that keeps posts consistent.

Open with the question

Start each post with the problem or observation that made the piece worth writing.

Show the reasoning

Explain the decisions, not just the final conclusion, so the post reflects your thinking style.

Close with a takeaway

End with one clear insight that someone could remember even after skimming.
